Journey Into Dante's World

Context, factions (Guelphs/Ghibellines, Whites/Blacks), exile, power structures shaping Dante’s worldview.

Aristotelian/Thomistic frameworks intersecting with poetic allegory and theological imagination.

Station 6: Research Methodology & Critical Analysis

Investigate, annotate, evaluate sources, and build an argument.
